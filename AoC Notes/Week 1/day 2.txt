First Column - Opponents Played Hand
<A> for Rock, <B> for Paper, and <C> for Scissors.

Second Column - Your Played Hand
<X> for Rock, <Y> for Paper, and <Z> for Scissors.

Var:
Total_Score - Sum of your score for each round
Shape_Selected - Rock = 1, Paper = 3, Scissors = 3
Round_Outcome - 0 if you lose / +3 if draw / +6 if you win
